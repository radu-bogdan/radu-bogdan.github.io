About Me

I am a PostDoc at the Radon instutute for computational and applied mathematics (RICAM) in Linz, Austria.

I received my PhD in applied mathematics from TU Darmstadt on “Finite element mass lumping for H(div) and H(curl)" under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Herbert Egger.

Research Interest

I am an expert in finite element methods with mass lumping. More details to follow ;)
